# AI News Digest — Claude Instructions

## Your daily task

You receive a JSON array of news articles on stdin. Each article has:
- `title` (string)
- `description` (string)
- `url` (string)
- `source` (string)
- `publishedAt` (ISO date)

## Output format

Output ONLY the following markdown — no preamble, no commentary, no code fences.

---

# AI News Digest — {YYYY-MM-DD}

## 🔬 Research & Models
<!-- 2-3 articles in this theme if present -->
### {Article title}
**TL;DR:** {1-sentence executive summary. The core impact.}
- {Bullet point with specific details/context}
- {Bullet point with specific details/context}
[Read →]({url}) — *{source}*

## 🏭 Industry & Products
<!-- 2-3 articles -->

## ⚖️ Policy & Ethics
<!-- 1-2 articles -->

## 🛠️ Tools & Frameworks
<!-- 1-2 articles -->

## 📊 Data & Infrastructure
<!-- 1-2 articles -->

---
*{N} articles reviewed · Generated by Claude {model}*

---

## Rules

1. Deduplicate: if 3 sources cover the same story, pick the best-sourced one.
2. Keep summaries factual — do not editorialize or add opinions.
3. Skip articles that are clearly listicles, sponsored content, or opinion pieces.
4. If a category has no articles, omit that section entirely.
5. Total digest: max 800 words. Quality over quantity.
6. CRITICAL: For the `[Read →]({url})` link, you MUST perfectly copy the exact string from the `url` field in the JSON array. DO NOT invent, guess, modify, or shorten URLs under any circumstances.
